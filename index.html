How it works

  • Choose your ride

    Select the type of ride you need, whether it's a standard ride, shared ride, or premium ride.

  • Set your pickoff and dropoff locations

    Enter the addresses for your pickup and drop-off locations, and our system will calculate the estimated fare for your ride.

  • Set your ride preferences

    Choose your preferred ride options, such as a quiet ride, a female driver, or a specific type of vehicle.

  • Book your ride

    Once you've entered all your ride details, simply confirm your booking and your ride will be on its way!

  • Track your ride in real time

    With our real-time tracking feature, you can see exactly where your ride is and when it will arrive at your pickup location.

  • Enjoy the ride

    Sit back, relax, and enjoy the comfortable and convenient ride to your destination.

  • Rate your driver and leave feedback

    After your ride is complete, you'll have the opportunity to rate your driver and leave feedback about your experience. Your feedback helps us improve our service and ensure that we're always meeting your needs
